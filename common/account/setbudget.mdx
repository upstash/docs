---
title: Set Budget
---

You can set a budget to be informed when your threshold is exceeded. Open the
Budget page in left menu under `Account > Budget`. Set your monthly budget, an
email will be sent to your email address when your total monthly cost exceeds
the budget amount.

<Frame><img src="/img/setbudget/setbudget.png"/></Frame>

> We will not stop your service when the cost reaches the budget. We will inform
> you so you can take an action.
