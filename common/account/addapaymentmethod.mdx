---
title: Add a Payment Method
---

Upstash does not require credit card for Free database. But paid databases, you
need to add at least one payment method. To add a payment, click on your profil
on top right then click `Account` then click `Billing` tab. In the screen click
on `Add Your Card` button. Then enter your name and credit card information the
following form:

<Frame><img src="/img/addpaymentmethod/ccform.png"/></Frame>

You can enter multiple credit cards and set one of them as the default one. The
payments will be charged from the default credit card.

<Frame><img src="/img/addpaymentmethod/cardlist.png" /></Frame>

## Payment Security

Upstash does not store users' credit card information in its servers. We use
Stripe Inc payment processing company to handle payments. You can read more
about payment security in Stripe
[here](https://stripe.com/docs/security/stripe).
